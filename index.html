Bibliogon - Write, Organize, Publish

Open-source authoring platform for books, articles, comics, and picture-books. A rich TipTap editor, a per-book Story Bible, drag-and-drop storyboards, and a KDP publishing wizard. Export to EPUB, PDF, DOCX, HTML, Markdown, and LaTeX, generate audiobooks, and sync chapters via Git. Installable, offline-capable PWA, local-first, AI-assisted. Free and open source.

Phylax - Your Living Medical Profile

Privacy-first health-record PWA for a living medical profile of observations, lab values, supplement plans, and open questions. Local-first and zero-knowledge: everything stays on your device, encrypted with AES-256-GCM and PBKDF2. No cloud, no accounts. Manage your own and a proxy profile, structure entries with AI assistance via your own key (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, LM Studio), and export rich PDF reports. Installable, offline-capable PWA, DE/EN. Free and open source.

Topos - Personal Inventory Tracker

Offline-first PWA and desktop app for indexing physical storage - file folders, archive boxes, and drawers - and everything inside them. A hierarchical category tree, priority levels, and pending-action tracking (review, cancel, archive). Import from Excel, search full-text, and recognise box contents from a photo with AI vision (Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini). Plugin-driven on PluginForge, available in 8 languages. Installable, offline-capable PWA plus desktop launchers for Linux, macOS, and Windows. Free and open source.
